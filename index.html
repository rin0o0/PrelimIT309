About Us

Our Story

Embarking on a journey amidst the challenging backdrop of the pandemic, "Super Daddy" emerged with humble beginnings, initially establishing a in-house store in Bulacan, Philippines. Fuelled by passion and a commitment to providing exceptional coffee, milk tea, and frappes, our brand swiftly gained recognition for its unique blend of flavors and dedication to quality. Within the span of a year, Super Daddy underwent a remarkable transformation, transitioning from a standalone cart to a franchise powerhouse. Today, our presence resonates across various dynamic cities, including Malabon, Manila, Caloocan, Marikina, and Las Pinas.
